Attendance Calculator — For KLU, FAST, NUST, COMSATS & All Universities

This tool calculates your current attendance percentage and tells you exactly how many more classes you can miss — or how many you must attend to recover. It works for any university's attendance system: just set the required attendance percentage to match your university's policy. Most Pakistani universities including KLU (Kalinga University), FAST-NUCES, NUST, COMSATS, UET, and Punjab University require a minimum of 75%.

How Many Classes Can I Bunk? — The Formula

Your attendance percentage = (classes attended ÷ total classes held) × 100. When you bunk a class, the total increases by 1 but your attended count stays the same — so your percentage drops each time. This tool simulates that drop and tells you the exact number of classes you can safely miss before falling below your required threshold.

Attendance Shortage — How to Recover

If your attendance is already below 75%, the calculator switches to Recovery Mode automatically. It calculates exactly how many consecutive classes you must attend to bring your percentage back up. Each attended class adds 1 to both your attended count and total — so recovery requires attending a run of classes without missing any.

75% Attendance Rule — Pakistani Universities

The 75% minimum attendance rule is enforced at most Pakistani universities as a condition for exam eligibility. If your attendance falls below 75% in any subject, you may be barred from sitting in the final exam for that subject. Some departments or courses require 80–85%. Always check your course outline or ask your department — and use the Required % field above to match your exact requirement.
