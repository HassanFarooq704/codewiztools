Why Track Your University Bunk Margin Balance?

Most higher education institutes and management systems enforce a strict 75% or 80% attendance threshold profile to let engineering, computer science, and medical students sit in final semester examination sessions. Falling below this baseline margin leads to direct portal blocks or course registration shortfalls.

Our script evaluates the mathematical matrix threshold between your attended class variables and the cumulative lecture tracks conducted by your college dashboard or university system. It calculates the consecutive safe slots available for bunking or how many successive classes you need to attend to satisfy the threshold balance seamlessly.

Optimizing Your Semester Attendance Routine
