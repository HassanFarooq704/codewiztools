Where does Lorem Ipsum come from?

Contrary to popular belief, Lorem Ipsum is not simply random text. It traces back to classical Latin literature written in 45 BC — specifically, a work by the Roman statesman Cicero called De Finibus Bonorum et Malorum (On the Ends of Good and Evil). The standard Lorem Ipsum passage starting with "Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet…" is a scrambled, altered excerpt from this text, making it over 2,000 years old.

What does Lorem Ipsum mean in English?

The Lorem Ipsum passage is intentionally scrambled Latin and has no direct, coherent English meaning. However, the original Cicero source translates roughly as:

Original Latin → English (approximate)

"Nor again is there anyone who loves, pursues, or desires to obtain pain of itself, because it is pain — but because occasionally circumstances occur in which toil and pain can procure him some great pleasure."

The words "Lorem ipsum" themselves are a corruption of the Latin phrase "dolorem ipsum", meaning "pain itself." The scrambling removed direct meaning intentionally so the text reads naturally without conveying content.
