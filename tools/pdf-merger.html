Why Use Our Online PDF Joiner?

Managing academic documents can be overwhelming. Whether you are a university student preparing a final thesis, a researcher compiling multiple study reports, or a developer organizing documentation, our free online PDF merger simplifies your workflow. You don't need to install heavy software like Adobe Acrobat or register on tracking websites.

Perfect for University Assignments & Portals

Most university student portals (like LMS or Moodle) allow only a single file submission per assignment. If you have a separate cover page, main report, and appendix, you must combine them. Our tool lets you upload all parts, arrange them in the exact sequence using up/down controls, and merge them with 100% precision.

100% Client-Side Privacy (Zero Server Uploads)

Unlike other online PDF tools that upload your sensitive documents to their clouds, CodeWizTools operates entirely in your web browser. The merging process runs locally via advanced JavaScript web buffers. Your files never leave your computer, ensuring total confidentiality for data-sensitive documents.
