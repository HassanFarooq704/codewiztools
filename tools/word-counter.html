Free Online Word Counter & Text Analyzer

Type or paste any text to instantly count words, characters (with and without spaces), sentences, paragraphs, and estimated reading time. Our engine uses advanced regex to filter out white spaces and deliver precise metrics in real-time.

This tool is ideal for writers, students, and SEO professionals working within strict character limits — such as X/Twitter (280 chars), Google Meta Descriptions (160 chars), or LinkedIn posts (3,000 chars). The estimated reading time is calculated based on a standard speed of 200 words per minute.
