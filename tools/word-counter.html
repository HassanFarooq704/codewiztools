Free Online Word Counter & Character Counter

This word counter analyzes your text in real time — every keystroke updates the word count, character count, sentence count, paragraph count, and estimated reading time instantly. There is no word limit or character limit. Paste an entire book chapter, a blog post, a social media caption, or a single sentence — the tool handles all of it.

Everything runs in your browser. Your text is never sent to any server, making this tool safe for confidential drafts, client documents, and private writing.

What Does the Word Counter Measure?

  • Words — any sequence of characters separated by whitespace
  • Characters — every character including spaces, tabs, and line breaks
  • Characters without spaces — only visible characters, no whitespace
  • Sentences — counted by terminal punctuation: . ! ?
  • Paragraphs — blocks of text separated by empty lines
  • Reading time — estimated at 200 words per minute, rounded up

Word Counter for SEO — Meta Titles and Descriptions

Google displays roughly 50–60 characters for a page title and 155–160 characters for a meta description before truncating with "...". Paste your SEO title or meta description into this tool and watch the character count update in real time. Staying within these limits ensures your full message appears in search results without being cut off.

Word Counter for Writers — Blog Posts, Essays, Social Media

Different content types have different ideal lengths. Blog posts that rank well on Google typically range from 1,500 to 2,500 words. LinkedIn posts get the most reach at 1,300–2,000 characters. X/Twitter limits each post to 280 characters. Use the platform limits reference above alongside the live counter to hit your target length every time.
