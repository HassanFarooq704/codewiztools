Free CGPA Calculator — Semester GPA & Cumulative CGPA on 4.0 Scale

This calculator has two modes. Use the Semester GPA tab to calculate your GPA for a single semester by entering each subject with its credit hours and grade. Use the Cumulative CGPA tab to combine multiple semester GPAs into your overall CGPA. Both use the standard 4.0 scale used by most Pakistani and international universities.

How GPA is Calculated — The Formula

GPA = Sum of (Grade Points × Credit Hours) ÷ Total Credit Hours

Example: If you scored A (4.0) in a 3-credit subject and B (3.0) in a 2-credit subject:

GPA = ((4.0 × 3) + (3.0 × 2)) ÷ (3 + 2) = (12 + 6) ÷ 5 = 3.60

How to Convert CGPA to Percentage

The most common formula used by Pakistani universities: Percentage = CGPA × 25. For example, CGPA 3.5 = 87.5%. Some universities use CGPA × 24 or other multipliers — check your university's official policy for the exact conversion.

CGPA Requirements at Pakistani Universities

  • CGPA 3.5 – 4.0: President's Honor Roll, Gold Medal, or Distinction at most universities
  • CGPA 3.0 – 3.49: Dean's List or Silver Medal
  • CGPA 2.5 – 2.99: Good standing
  • CGPA 2.0 – 2.49: Satisfactory — meeting minimum requirements
  • Below CGPA 2.0: Academic probation at many universities including FAST, NUST, COMSATS
