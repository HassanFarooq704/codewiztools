Free Online Percentage Calculator — 4 Calculation Modes

This percentage calculator covers the four most common percentage calculations in one place. Results update on every keystroke — no need to click a Calculate button. Everything runs in your browser, no data is ever sent to any server.

How to Calculate Percentage — All 4 Formulas

  • What is X% of Y? — Use: (X ÷ 100) × Y. Example: 15% of 200 = 30. Used for discounts, tax, tips, and commission.
  • X is what % of Y? — Use: (X ÷ Y) × 100. Example: 30 is 15% of 200. Used for test scores, completion rates, and market share.
  • Percentage change: — Use: ((B − A) ÷ |A|) × 100. A positive result is increase, negative is decrease. Used for price changes, growth tracking.
  • Add/subtract percentage: — Adding: Y × (1 + X/100). Subtracting: Y × (1 − X/100). Used for price after tax, salary after deduction.

Percentage Calculator for Students — Marks and Grades

To convert marks to a percentage: enter your obtained marks in the first field and total marks in the second field of the "X is what % of Y?" calculator. For example: scored 67 out of 80 = (67 ÷ 80) × 100 = 83.75%. Use the "Percentage Increase/Decrease" calculator to see how much your grade improved between two tests.

Percentage Calculator for GST, VAT and Tax

To calculate a price including tax: use Add Percentage mode — enter the tax rate (e.g. 17 for 17% GST in Pakistan, 20 for 20% UK VAT) and the pre-tax amount. To find the original price before tax: use Subtract Percentage with the same tax rate and the total amount.
